Services C1 — Help1RVA: What It Is, How It Works, Its Limitations

Research Help1RVA (help1rva.com) as the primary service directory infrastructure for Richmond's immigrant and refugee communities.

Questions to answer:
1. What is Help1RVA? Who owns and operates it? When was it created? How is it funded?
2. How many organizations and services are listed? What are the main service categories?
3. What is the user interface like for a resident with limited English? What barriers does the interface create?
4. Is there a public API or structured data export? What data format is available?
5. Does Help1RVA have multilingual support? If so, which languages?
6. What is the process for organizations to add or update their listings? How current is the data?
7. Is there a mobile app or SMS interface?
8. What privacy statement or data handling disclosure is present?
9. How does Help1RVA relate to FindHelp/Aunt Bertha and UniteUs? Are they the same data, different data, or competing systems?
10. Has Help1RVA been the subject of any evaluation or improvement planning that is publicly documented?

Output:
- Facts (with URLs)
- Inferences (clearly labeled)
- Assessment: what can a hackathon team build using Help1RVA data vs. what requires going around it?
- What a hackathon team should verify before demo (phone number accuracy, language flags, service currency)
