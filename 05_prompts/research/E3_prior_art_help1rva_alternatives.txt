Prior Art E3 — FindHelp Competitors, 211, and Similar Service Directory Platforms

Research the national landscape of community resource directory platforms that could serve as alternatives to, improvements of, or complements to Help1RVA.

Platforms to research:
1. 211.org / United Way 211 (including Virginia 211)
2. FindHelp / Aunt Bertha (findhelp.org)
3. UniteUs
4. NowPow (now part of UniteUs)
5. CareDx / CareMore
6. Healthify (now merged with WellSky Social Determinants)
7. Expound (if relevant)
8. Open Referral / HSDS (Human Service Data Specification) standard

For each:
- What is the platform and who uses it?
- Is it used in Richmond or Virginia?
- What does the resident-facing experience look like for a non-English speaker?
- What is the privacy model?
- Is there public API access?
- Is it interoperable with other platforms?
- What does it cost for an organization to participate?

Open Referral / HSDS specifically:
- What is the Open Referral initiative? What problem does it solve?
- Has Help1RVA adopted HSDS or any common data standard?
- Could a hackathon team use HSDS-formatted data if it exists?

Output:
- Comparison table of platforms
- What a hackathon team could realistically use in 48 hours from each platform
- The interoperability gap and what solving it would require
