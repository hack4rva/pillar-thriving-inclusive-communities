Top 10 Research Questions — Thriving and Inclusive Communities

For each question, provide a brief answer using verifiable public sources. Label each finding as Verified (with source), Inference, or Unknown.

1. How many foreign-born residents live in Richmond, Virginia, and what are the top 5 countries of origin? What percentage have limited English proficiency?

2. How many refugees were resettled in the Richmond metro area in each of the past 5 years? Which resettlement agencies operate in Richmond?

3. What is Help1RVA? How does it work? What categories of services does it list? Is there a public API or data export available?

4. What languages does the City of Richmond provide translation or interpretation services in, and for which departments?

5. What communication tools and channels are most commonly used by Spanish-speaking immigrant communities in mid-size Southern US cities for sharing community information?

6. What are the HIPAA and state privacy law constraints on sharing client intake data between City agencies and nonprofit service providers in Virginia?

7. What national tools or platforms have successfully served immigrant and refugee populations with service discovery, and what design patterns made them work?

8. What is the current status of FindHelp (Aunt Bertha) and UniteUs in Richmond? Which local organizations use them? Are they interoperable with Help1RVA?

9. What is the City of Richmond's current language access policy? Does it meet requirements under Title VI of the Civil Rights Act?

10. What data about service usage or unmet need exists for immigrant and refugee residents in Richmond? What organizations collect it?
