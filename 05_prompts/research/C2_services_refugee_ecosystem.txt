Services C2 — The Richmond Refugee and Immigrant Service Ecosystem

Map the organizations that provide services to immigrant and refugee residents in Richmond, Virginia. Use only publicly available information.

For each organization category, identify:
- Key organizations in Richmond
- Primary services offered
- Languages served
- Whether immigration documentation is required
- Public contact information
- Public notes on intake or eligibility

Organization categories to research:
1. Refugee resettlement agencies (ReEstablish Richmond, others)
2. Legal aid organizations serving immigrant residents
3. Healthcare providers with immigrant/refugee focus
4. ESL and literacy programs
5. Workforce development programs serving this population (YMCA, community colleges, workforce boards)
6. Housing and rental assistance programs
7. Food assistance programs
8. Mental health and trauma-informed services
9. Faith-based organizations with publicly listed immigrant services
10. Cultural and community associations that provide navigation support

For Richmond specifically:
- What organizations are listed on Help1RVA for immigrant/refugee-relevant services?
- What organizations are mentioned in the rubric working session materials?

Output:
- Organization list organized by category
- Gaps: which service categories appear underserved in Richmond's public-facing ecosystem?
- Language capacity map: which communities (Spanish, Arabic, Nepali, Burmese) have more or less coverage?
