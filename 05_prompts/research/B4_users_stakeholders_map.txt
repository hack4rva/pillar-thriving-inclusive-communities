Users B4 — Stakeholder Map for Thriving and Inclusive Communities

Map all stakeholders involved in or affected by immigrant and refugee service access in Richmond.

For each stakeholder category, describe:
- Their role in the current service ecosystem
- Their interests (what they want to see happen)
- Their concerns (what they are worried about)
- Their capacity to participate in a hackathon prototype or its continuation

Stakeholder categories to map:
1. Immigrant and refugee residents (primary beneficiaries)
2. Nonprofit case managers and social workers
3. Trusted community organizations (faith, cultural, advocacy)
4. City of Richmond social services and housing staff
5. Workforce development programs (YMCA, community colleges, workforce boards)
6. Help1RVA platform operators
7. FindHelp / UniteUs platform representatives
8. Legal aid organizations serving immigrant residents
9. Healthcare providers serving immigrant and refugee communities
10. Potential hackathon champions (ReEstablish Richmond, YMCA, NCS — as identified in rubric materials)

Output: A stakeholder table with columns: Stakeholder, Role, Interests, Concerns, Hackathon participation potential.
Then: "Top 3 stakeholders a hackathon team should try to reach during the weekend."
