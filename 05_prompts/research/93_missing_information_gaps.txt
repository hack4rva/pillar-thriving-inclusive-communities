Missing Information Gaps — Thriving and Inclusive Communities

This prompt maps what information is currently missing and needed for hackathon teams to build well. For each gap, assess: how critical it is, whether it can be filled by research, and what the team should do if it cannot be filled.

GAPS TO ASSESS:

1. Help1RVA data structure and export
- What format is Help1RVA data available in?
- Is there an API or structured export?
- What fields are included for each organization listing?
- Criticality: HIGH — affects all service directory tools

2. Priority languages for Richmond immigrant population
- What languages beyond Spanish are most common among Richmond's immigrant and refugee population?
- Which languages do Richmond service organizations actually serve?
- Criticality: HIGH — affects multilingual tool design

3. Communication channel usage patterns
- Which communication channels (WhatsApp, SMS, Facebook, in-person) are most commonly used by each language community in Richmond?
- Who are the trusted information intermediaries in each community?
- Criticality: HIGH — affects trusted messenger tool design

4. Organizations that explicitly serve undocumented residents
- Which Richmond-area organizations have explicit policies about not requiring immigration documentation?
- Where is this documented publicly?
- Criticality: HIGH — affects "no papers required" content flags

5. Cross-agency referral pathway
- What is the actual sequence of organizations a resident visits when navigating housing, workforce, and reentry support?
- Where do handoffs happen and where do residents get lost?
- Criticality: CRITICAL for Statement 2, but no dataset exists — must conceptualize without data

6. Help1RVA improvement roadmap
- Is Help1RVA actively planning multilingual improvements?
- Who owns Help1RVA and is that person interested in hackathon collaboration?
- Criticality: MEDIUM — affects framing of Concept 6 (interface improvement)

7. Current service capacity and wait times
- Which organizations are currently accepting new clients?
- What are typical wait times for housing, workforce, and legal services?
- Criticality: MEDIUM — affects usefulness of case manager coordination tool

For each gap, provide:
- Current state of available information
- Whether research can partially fill the gap
- What the team should assume or do if the gap cannot be filled before demo
